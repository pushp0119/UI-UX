Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

When is TechFest 2025?

TechFest 2025 will be held from March 15-17, 2025 at our college campus in New Delhi.

Who can participate in TechFest?

TechFest is open to all undergraduate and postgraduate students from recognized colleges and universities across India. Both individual and team participation is allowed depending on the event.

How do I register for events?

You can register online through our registration page. Fill in your details, select the events you want to participate in, and submit the form. You will receive a confirmation email within 24 hours.

Is there a registration fee?

Registration is free for all participants. However, some events may require a nominal fee for materials or equipment, which will be mentioned during registration.

Can I participate in multiple events?

Yes, you can participate in multiple events as long as the schedules don't conflict. Please check the schedule page to plan accordingly.

What are the prizes for winners?

Winners will receive cash prizes, certificates, and trophies. Prize amounts vary by event, with major competitions offering prizes up to ₹75,000. Check the schedule page for previous year results and prize details.

Is accommodation provided?

Accommodation can be arranged for outstation participants at a nominal cost. Please mention your accommodation requirement during registration, and our team will contact you with details.

What should I bring to the event?

Bring your college ID card, laptop (if required for your events), and any specific equipment mentioned for your registered events. We'll provide a detailed list in the confirmation email.

How can I get updates about TechFest?

Follow us on our social media channels (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter) for regular updates. You can also subscribe to our newsletter or check the website regularly for announcements.

Can I volunteer for TechFest?

Yes! We welcome volunteers. Please contact us at volunteer@techfest2025.in or fill out the contact form with "Volunteer" as the subject.

Where can I see photos from previous events?

Check out our Gallery page to see photos from TechFest 2024 and previous years. You can also follow us on Instagram for live updates during the event.

What if I have more questions?

Feel free to contact us through the contact form above, email us at info@techfest2025.in, or call us at +91 98765 43210. Our team is available to help you Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 6 PM IST.
