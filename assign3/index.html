Welcome to TechFest 2025

Join us for the most exciting annual technical festival of the year! TechFest 2025 brings together students, innovators, and tech enthusiasts from across India for three days of competitions, workshops, exhibitions, and networking opportunities.

This year's theme is "Innovation for Tomorrow" - showcasing cutting-edge technology, creative problem-solving, and collaborative learning. Whether you're a coding enthusiast, robotics expert, or design wizard, there's something for everyone at TechFest 2025.
